## Section 2 — Recommended Page Structure

### Two-Site Architecture

The materials confirm two distinct operations that should remain separate web presences:

- **hoffmansoftwater.com** — Pretreatment division. New-customer acquisition, lead generation. The audience is often encountering Hoffman for the first time through a search query.
- **hoffmanwater.com** — Parent company, existing relationships. Industrial water treatment (boilers, towers, wastewater, chemical programs). This site should exist but its redesign is a Phase 1B priority.

The sites should cross-link clearly and share a visual identity but serve different intents.

---

### hoffmansoftwater.com — Recommended Pages

#### Page 1: Homepage
**Goal:** Convert first-time visitors from search or referral into assessment requests.  
**Audience:** All segments; most often Segment A or B arriving from search.

#### Page 2: Services Overview
**Goal:** Show the full service breadth before routing to individual service pages.  
**Audience:** Prospects in early-evaluation stage; referrals trying to confirm scope.

#### Page 3: Water Softening — Commercial & Industrial
**Goal:** Capture high-intent searches ("commercial water softener Cincinnati," "industrial water softener Ohio"). Convert to site assessment requests.  
**Audience:** Segment A and B, specifically facilities with scale/boiler feed problems.

#### Page 4: Reverse Osmosis Systems
**Goal:** Capture "industrial reverse osmosis Ohio" searches. Convert pharma, food processing, and manufacturing leads.  
**Audience:** Segment A, specifically pharma/lab/food applications.

#### Page 5: Solid Chemistry Water Treatment
**Goal:** Differentiation page and SEO target ("solid chemistry water treatment," "SOLIDified system Ohio"). Convert facility managers seeking liquid chemical replacement.  
**Audience:** Segment C (EHS/Sustainability) and Segment A (Industrial Facilities).

#### Page 6: Filtration & Specialty Treatment
**Goal:** Catch-all for multimedia filtration, carbon, and custom applications. Convert facilities with non-standard requirements.  
**Audience:** Segment A, specialty manufacturing.

#### Page 7: Industries We Serve (hub) or individual industry landing pages
**Goal:** Serve industry-specific SEO targets. Confirm relevance to specific verticals. Build topical authority.  
**Audience:** Segment-specific (food processors, pharma, hospitality, etc.).

#### Page 8: About / Doug Hoffman
**Goal:** Build trust through the founder story and company history. Reduce friction for first-time buyers who need a person behind the brand.  
**Audience:** All segments, especially Segment B and E evaluating vendor credibility.

#### Page 9: Case Studies / Results
**Goal:** Provide social proof at the decision stage. Convert comparison shoppers who need to see real results.  
**Audience:** Segment B and E (decision-makers comparing vendors).

#### Page 10: Contact / Request Assessment
**Goal:** Primary lead capture page. Convert phone calls and form submissions.  
**Audience:** All segments at decision stage.

---

### hoffmanwater.com — Recommended Pages (Phase 1B)

The parent company site needs to exist as a credible, current presence. Current scope of work is minimal — focus on establishing authority rather than generating new leads directly.

1. Homepage — industrial water treatment overview
2. Services (boilers, cooling towers, closed loops, wastewater, specialty chemicals)
3. Solid Chemistry programs (overlap with Soft Water site, linked)
