What Is Solid Chemistry

State-of-the-Art Chemistry. None of the Drums.

Hoffman Water's solid chemistry water treatment uses proven chemical combinations manufactured into a solid concentrate. Applied at your site using AP Tech's patented SOLIDified dissolving equipment, the products deliver the same corrosion control, scale inhibition, and microbiological protection as liquid programs — without the 55-gallon drums, the handling risk, or the diesel footprint.

Cooling towers, boilers, and closed loop systems receive the benefit of a well-run chemical program using proven chemistry, without the hassles of dealing with liquids. Whether you're pursuing LEED certification or looking for ways to make your facility safer and more environmentally friendly while reducing costs, solid chemistry water treatment can help.

The Difference

Liquid Drums vs. Solid Chemistry

Before: Liquid Chemical Programs
  • 55-gallon drums stacked in mechanical rooms
  • 500 lb drums require heavy equipment or injury risk
  • Hazardous liquid discharge risk during shipment and storage
  • Dangerous odors and fumes in mechanical rooms
  • Eye wash station required by OSHA
  • Sodium Hydroxide for pH control — highly corrosive
After: Solid Chemistry Programs
  • Compact wall-mounted SOLIDified dispensing system
  • 44-lb cases — safely handled by one person
  • No hazardous liquid discharge risk
  • No odors, no fumes, cleaner mechanical rooms
  • Eye wash station no longer required
  • No Sodium Hydroxide — safer chemistry
Why Switch

Three Reasons Facilities Convert to Solid Chemistry

Safety Benefits

  • Remove the risk of discharging hazardous liquid chemicals into the environment during shipment, storage, or application
  • Improve operator safety by reducing exposure to hazardous chemicals
  • Eliminate dangerous and offensive odors or fumes
  • Smaller profile products are safe and simple to apply — safer, cleaner mechanical rooms

Sustainability Benefits

  • 55-gallon drums consume 2.5 more gallons of diesel per shipment vs. a 44-lb case of solids with equivalent active ingredients
  • Fully recyclable packaging
  • No Sodium Hydroxide required for pH control (highly corrosive and energy-intensive to produce)
  • Supports LEED certification pathways

Economic Benefits

  • Maximize utility efficiency and equipment life by controlling corrosion, scale deposits, and microbiological fouling
  • Water savings from increased cycles of concentration (no triple-rinsing of drums)
  • Reduce costs from chemical exposure incidents and drum handling injuries
  • Eliminate eye wash station installation and maintenance costs
Applications

Complete Programs for Every System

Solid chemistry programs are engineered for the systems that demand consistent, reliable chemistry.

Cooling Towers

Corrosion inhibitors, scale inhibitors, and biocides in solid form — precise dosing, consistent cycles of concentration, no drum handling.

Boilers

Oxygen scavengers, scale and corrosion inhibitors formulated as solid concentrate — reliable feed without liquid chemical storage.

Closed Loop Systems

Corrosion protection for closed heating and cooling loops — solid chemistry delivers the same performance as liquid programs with a fraction of the logistics complexity.
