The Problem

Your Water Has More in It Than Hardness

Hard water scale gets most of the attention, but Ohio's industrial water supply carries a range of contaminants that damage equipment, compromise product quality, and shorten the life of downstream treatment systems. Suspended solids foul membranes. Chlorine degrades resin and seals. Iron stains and clogs. Left untreated, these issues compound — a softener working against iron-laden water wears out early, and an RO membrane without proper pretreatment needs replacement in months instead of years.

Filtration is often the foundation everything else depends on.

  • Suspended Solids & Turbidity

    Particles foul downstream equipment, clog softener resin, and reduce system efficiency.

  • Chlorine & Chloramines

    Municipal chlorine attacks rubber seals, softener resin, and membranes, reducing system lifespan.

  • Iron & Manganese

    Stain equipment and piping, foul resin, and create operational headaches across water-using systems.

  • Application-Specific Contaminants

    Food processing, pharma, and manufacturing have water chemistry requirements that generic softening alone can't address.

Our Services

Filtration Systems We Engineer and Install

Each system is selected based on your water analysis and what's downstream. We match the media to the problem.

Multimedia Filtration

Multi-bed filter media (sand, gravel, anthracite) removes suspended solids, turbidity, and particulates from water before it reaches your softener, membrane, or process equipment.

Best for
Boiler feed pretreatment, RO pretreatment, general process water cleanup

Activated Carbon

Removes chlorine, chloramines, taste, odor, and organic compounds that damage downstream equipment and affect product quality. Critical upstream of softeners and membranes.

Best for
Chlorinated municipal supplies, food & beverage, pharmaceutical equipment protection

Iron, Manganese & Specialty

Iron removal, manganese treatment, and application-specific media for facilities with elevated metals or non-standard water chemistry. Designed from the water analysis up.

Best for
Well water, facilities with staining, manufacturing with specific purity needs
